I’m a Senior Cloud Solution Architect at Microsoft South Africa, sitting inside the Customer Success Unit out of Johannesburg. My day job is helping large enterprise and regulated customers design Azure platforms that hold up: hybrid landing zones, sovereign and air-gapped deployments, network architectures with serious blast-radius constraints, and Well-Architected Framework reviews when something is already on fire.

Before Microsoft I spent two decades on the customer side of the table — building, breaking, and rebuilding infrastructure for telcos, iGaming, and financial services. That mileage is the reason architectural advice from me tends to come with a strong opinion and a war story attached.
